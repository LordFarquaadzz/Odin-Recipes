Red Beans and Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Uncooked White Rice
  • 1 Pound of Red Kidney Beans
  • Left over Ham-bone (preferably with some ham scraps still intact)
  • 1 Large Yellow/Brown Onion
  • 3-4 Stalks of Celery
  • 1 Whole Green Bell Pepper
  • 3-4 Green Onions
  • 2 Tablespoons of Butter

Steps

  1. Start by soaking your red beans in water for 12-24 hours
  2. When beans have been soaked and you are ready to cook, start by heating a large pot on medium heat
  3. Add enough flavorless oil to the pot to cover the bottom and add your trinity
  4. Continue to cook on medium heat until the clerey, bell pepper and onion are translucent
  5. When the celery, green pepper and onion are cooked through, add your soaked red beans and cover with water
  6. Turn heat to medium-high and bring mixture to a low boil
  7. Add your ham-bone to the pot and allow mixture to cook until beans begin to soften
  8. Depending on how you like your beans, cooking times will be different
  9. While your beans are cooking, start cooking your rice
  10. Begin by washing your rice
  11. In a medium pot, melt 2 tablespoons of butter and add in your uncooked rice
  12. Allow rice to toast, stirring occasionally to avoid burning
  13. Once the rice has been toasted, cover the rice with water and bring to a boil
  14. When water begins to boil, add 1 tablespoon of salt and cover the pot with a lid
  15. Turn heat to medium-low until the all the water is absorbed

Time to Serve!

  1. Scoop your rice onto a plate
  2. Cover rice with a good amount of your red beans mixture
  3. Garnish with chopped green onions
  4. Enjoy!
