Red Lentil Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of Red Lentils (uncooked)
  • 12oz Coconut Milk
  • 2 Tablespoons of Ghee (or any clarified butter)
  • 2 Tablespoons of Curry Powder
  • 2 Tablespoons Garlic
  • 2 Tablespoons Ginger Root
  • 2 Tablespoons of Tomato Paste
  • 24oz of Veggie Broth
  • 1 Large Yellow Onion
  • 1 Bunch of Cilantro
  • 3-4 Handfuls of Raw Spinach (optional)

Steps

  1. Grate your onion, garlic and ginger and keep separate
  2. In a large pot, add your ghee and heat to medium
  3. Once ghee has completely melted, add your onion, garlic, ginger and curry powder
  4. Stir the mixture and do not allow it to burn
  5. Once the onion is cooked down, garlic is fragrant and the spices are toasted, add in your tomato paste and continue to stir
  6. Turn heat to low and stir occasionally making sure not to burn any ingredients
  7. In a strainer, rinse your pound of red lentils and your spinach
  8. Once rinsed, add your lentils to the pot and stir
  9. Now add in your vegetable broth and bring to a low boil
  10. Once mixture is at a low boil, stir in your spinach and cover the pot with a lid and turn the heat to medium-low

Time to Serve!

  1. Scoop your lentil mixture onto a plate or into a bowl
  2. Serve with white rice or naan
  3. Garnish lentils with cilantro
  4. Enjoy!
