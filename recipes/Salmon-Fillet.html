Salmon Fillet with Soy Ginger Marinade

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Pounds of Salmon sliced into fillets
  • 2-3 Green Onions
  • 3-4 Tablespoons of Miso Paste
  • 2 Tablespoons of Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons of Sesame Oil
  • 2 Tablespoons of Rice Wine Vinegar
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Large Knob of Ginger Root
  • Sesame Seeds for Garnish

Steps

  1. Place your salmon fillets in a large dish or ziplock bag
  2. To the bag, add your miso paste, soy sauce sesame oil and rice wine vinegar
  3. Grate your garlic and ginger and add to salmon
  4. Roughly chop your green onions and add to salmon
  5. Mix well and let salmon marinade for 24 hours
  6. After 24 hours, remove salmon fillets and sear both sides
  7. Continue to cook salmon skin side down until middle is cooked through

Time to Serve!

  1. Plate your salmon fillet
  2. Serve with some white rice
  3. Garnish with some toasted sesame seeds
  4. Enjoy!
